Waterfall charts are an alternative to pie charts for displaying distributions.

However when it's time to compare the size of categories and sub-categories, waterfall are the most convenient representation. While in a pie-chart you would have to compare wedges at different angles, in a waterfall chart you just compare the size of bars as all bars are parallel to one another.
